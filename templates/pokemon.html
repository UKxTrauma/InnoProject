Interested in the pocket monsters?

Within the Pokémon universe, the little monster-like beings roam the world alongside humans!
Wether you are reading, playing or watching Pokémon media, you will always feel a sense of adventure and exploration!
There are currently 913 species of Pokémon split throughout the 9 generations with more to be announced from Scarlet & Violet later this year (2022).

In 1996 Satoshi Tajiri created his original work: Pocket Monsters Red and Green.
The pair of Game Boy video games were developed by Game Freak and published by Nintendo.
Following numerous remakes and spin-offs, the collection of Pokémon games continues to expand and you may click the link above to see the current list of released Pokémon games.

The TV Series of Pokémon is also always expanding, almost parallel to the extensive list of Pokémon movies! Feel free to use the links above to see the released media.
